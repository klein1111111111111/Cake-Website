ABOUT THE OWNER

Diana Nicdao David
Founder of Confiserie – Pastries and Bakeshop

Diana Nicdao David is the passionate founder behind Confiserie – Pastries and Bakeshop.
Her journey in the culinary world began with a Bachelor of Science degree in
Culinary Arts from the prestigious Leroux Culinary Academy located in SM Pampanga.

Her professional training started with an On-the-Job Training (OJT) at Le Chef
at the Manor, Baguio, where she honed her foundational skills in a real-world
culinary environment. She then gained her first job experience at Vikings
SM Pampanga, one of the country's renowned buffet chains, further sharpening
her expertise in large-scale food preparation and service.

With a strong foundation in the Philippines, Diana pursued her culinary dreams
abroad.Her talents and dedication led her to the Margarita Hotel Nashville
in Tennessee, United States of America, where she expanded her skills and embraced
international standards in hospitality and patisserie.

Today, Diana brings her diverse experience and love for the craft into every creation
at Confiserie, offering a delectable selection of pastries that reflect her journey, training, and unwavering passion for baking.
